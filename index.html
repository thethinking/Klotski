Klotski Puzzle
Puzzle History Customize Solution
One day on the end of Feb. 2019, I recalled the puzzle and told to my son, and then I implemented it in browser. During programming, I searched the background of Klotski puzzle from Wikipedia. Eventually I was attracted by the puzzle. I decided to establish a web page for it with purposes to make it convenient to describe a layout and share the solution.
Features:
1）Keyboard Support. Arrow key or ASDW to move the target block. "[" and "]" for prev and next round.
2）History，save the best record of each round, and show the solution in animation.
3）User-defined layout. Layout description：'0' for blank, '1' for small square, '2' for vertical rectangle, '3' for horizonal rectangle, '4' for the boss. A layout description consists of 20 numbers that corresponds to each cell from left to right and top to bottom. For example, the first round could be described as '24422442233221121001'.
4）Solutions，demonstrate or import or export the solution.
